# SAHM in STEM Objectives
A community for women with an interest in all things STEM and the rapidly growing world of AI, all while balancing the daily grind of staying or working at home with children. 

* Safe space for moms with an academic or professional background, or with a general interest in tech.
* Help mothers find projects, whether pro bono or paid, to help close the gap on their resumes.
* Find and share free or low-cost resources to refresh their knowledge and/or upskill via certifications and courses.
* Inform mothers about local or virtual job fairs (particularly those that target women).
* Provide scholarships and grants for mothers who want to further their education.
